Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2026. Replace bracketed placeholders before publishing.

This Privacy Policy explains how TrainerMate (“TrainerMate”, “we”, “us” or “our”) handles information when you use the TrainerMate website, desktop app and support services.

1. Who we are

Controller/contact: [Your legal trading name], [your business address], email support@trainermate.xyz. If you are unsure who controls particular course/delegate data in a provider portal, check the terms and privacy notices of that provider.

2. Information we may process

  • Account details such as NDORS trainer ID, email address, plan, status, app version and device identifier.
  • Support messages and diagnostic summaries you choose to send.
  • Provider configuration such as provider names and portal URLs.
  • Local course metadata shown inside TrainerMate, such as course title, date/time, provider and Zoom status.
  • Zoom authorisation metadata needed to connect your Zoom account. TrainerMate should not ask for your Zoom password.
  • Payment/subscription information if you upgrade. Card details should be processed by the payment provider, not stored by TrainerMate.

3. Local-first design

TrainerMate is designed to run locally on your computer. Provider credentials, Zoom tokens, local course cache, certificate cache and trainer documents should be stored locally where possible, using operating system protections such as the credential manager/keychain where available.

4. Why we use information

  • To provide the app and verify your account/plan.
  • To help you check provider portal courses and Zoom link status.
  • To provide support, troubleshooting and update notices.
  • To protect the service from misuse and keep audit records.
  • To comply with legal, tax and accounting obligations.

5. Lawful bases

Depending on the activity, we may rely on contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation or consent. For example, account/licensing is usually needed to perform the contract, while security logging may be a legitimate interest.

6. What we do not want you to send

Do not send provider passwords, Zoom passwords, full delegate lists, screenshots containing unnecessary personal data, payment card details, or special category information unless specifically requested and unavoidable for support.

7. Sharing

We may use trusted processors for hosting, email, support, payments, licensing, analytics that you have agreed to, and app distribution. We do not sell your personal data. Where Zoom or provider portals are used, their own terms and privacy policies also apply.

8. Retention

We keep account and support records only for as long as reasonably needed for the service, legal obligations, security, dispute handling and accounting. Local app data remains on your computer unless you delete it or send it to support.

9. Your rights

You may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to certain processing, and to complain to the ICO. You can contact us at support@trainermate.xyz. The ICO website provides more information about UK data protection rights.

10. Security

We use technical and organisational measures intended to protect information, including local-only dashboard binding, credential-store usage where possible, HTTPS for remote services, update checks with checksums where configured, CSRF protection and defensive headers.

11. International transfers

Some service providers, such as cloud, email, payment or Zoom services, may process data outside the UK. Where required, appropriate safeguards should be used.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The latest version will be published on this site.
