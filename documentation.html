TrainerMate Zoom Integration Guide

This guide explains how to connect, use, and remove Zoom integration within TrainerMate.

Adding the App

  1. Log in to the TrainerMate dashboard.
  2. Go to the Zoom Accounts section.
  3. Click Link Zoom Account.
  4. Sign in to Zoom and approve access.
  5. Your Zoom account will appear inside TrainerMate.

Using the App

  • Verify existing Zoom meetings
  • Create meetings where provider permissions allow
  • Prevent duplicate Zoom meeting creation
  • Sync meeting IDs, join links, and passwords

Removing the App

  1. Open Zoom Marketplace.
  2. Go to Manage → Added Apps.
  3. Find TrainerMate.
  4. Click Remove or Uninstall.
