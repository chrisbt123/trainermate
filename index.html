Careful with your information

You should not need to understand cybersecurity jargon to feel reassured. TrainerMate is designed to keep sensitive details limited, protected and out of sight wherever possible.

Passwords stay off the website

Provider login details are kept on your computer using Windows secure storage. TrainerMate does not ask you to send passwords to support.

Zoom uses its own sign-in screen

You approve TrainerMate through Zoom. You do not type your Zoom password into TrainerMate.

Account details are protected

Online account records are kept to the minimum needed for sign-in, plan checks and support, with extra protection around identifying details.
