Zoom deauthorisation

This page explains how to disconnect TrainerMate from your Zoom account.

Disconnect inside TrainerMate

  1. Open TrainerMate.
  2. Go to Zoom accounts.
  3. Choose the connected Zoom account.
  4. Select disconnect/remove if available.

Disconnect from Zoom

  1. Sign in to Zoom.
  2. Open the Zoom App Marketplace.
  3. Go to Manage / Installed Apps.
  4. Find TrainerMate.
  5. Remove or deauthorise the app.

What happens afterwards

After deauthorisation, TrainerMate should no longer be able to use Zoom OAuth tokens for your Zoom account. Locally cached connection details may remain on your computer until you remove the Zoom account from TrainerMate or delete local app data.

Support
